My name is Andy Tran and I'm... not in this picture. My mother on the other hand is. She was the little girl in the middle of her young siblings here: My uncle is on the left, my 8th Aunt is next, then comes my mom, my 6th, and 5th aunts. This picture was taken in Phan Thiet, before the Vietnam War. The photo was taken by my maternal grandfather, who was a self-taught photographer, using a German-made Rolleicord that he borrowed from a friend.

I'm using this as a simple practice in Web Developer, something I really love to do. Being an immigrant from Vietnam, my family is very important to me, so it is not by accident that I chose this as one of the topics for my websites. I'm sure the subject has been done to death, but every family is different, and this is only half of my big fat Vietnamese family: my mother's side.

Enjoy the little tour and feel free to contact me. Have a beautiful day.
